## Positive Semi definite(PSD)?
$$
\forall v\neq 0, \textit{v}^{T}A\textit{v} \geq 0
$$

## Positive definite(PD)?
$$
\forall v\neq 0, \textit{v}^{T}A\textit{v} \gt 0
$$

## |det L| = $|\Sigma|^{1/2}$?
$$
\Sigma = LL^T
$$
det(A^{T}) = det(A)
$$
det(\Sigma) = det(L)det(L^T) = det(L)det(L) = (det(L))^2
$$
|det L| = \sqrt{det(\Sigma)}
$$
